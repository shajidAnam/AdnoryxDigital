FAQ

Common questions before starting Meta ads

How much budget do I need to start Meta ads?

Many businesses start with $300 to $1,000 per month in ad spend. The right budget depends on your market, goal, and funnel quality.

How long before I see results?

You can see early performance signals within the first 2 to 4 weeks. Stronger, more stable results usually build over 60 to 90 days.

No. We support both Bangladesh-based businesses and international clients with remote collaboration.

Will you provide weekly reports?

Yes. You receive weekly updates with KPI trends, insights, and next actions in simple language.

Can you help with ad creatives?

Yes. We provide creative direction, test plans, and messaging guidance to improve conversion performance.

What if I already run campaigns?
